F.A.Q.

Is 8Bit-Arena free to join?

Yes! Browsing our library and playing our mini-games is 100% free.

Can I play the Trending Games here?

No, we provide reviews and direct links to official stores like Steam, PlayStation, and Epic Games where you can purchase them.

Does the Snake game save my high score?

Currently, the score resets when you refresh the page. We are working on a leaderboard update soon!

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via our Discord channel or Twitter linked in the footer.

Do you host esports tournaments?

We partner with major organizers to bring you tournament news. Check our "Community" tab for upcoming event schedules.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! 8Bit-Arena is fully responsive, so you can browse games and play the mini-game on your phone or tablet.

Can I submit my own game for review?

Yes, indie developers can submit their projects via the "Contact" form. Our editorial team reviews submissions weekly.

What are the PC requirements for these games?

By clicking "View Details" on any game card, you will be redirected to the official page which lists the specific system requirements.

Do you offer cheat codes or guides?

We focus on fair play and official reviews. However, our community Discord often shares strategy guides and walkthroughs.

How do I report a bug on the website?

If you encounter issues with the Snake game or navigation, please tweet at us or send a message through the contact section.
